Comprehensive Support for Startups

The programme offers a multi-faceted support system designed to address the critical needs of early-stage technology companies.

Financial Assistance

Up to HK$500,000 in performance-based funding over 24 months to cover essential operational needs.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Rent-free office and co-working spaces, plus complimentary access to state-of-the-art meeting rooms, conference facilities, and technology labs to foster collaboration and innovation.

Business Development

Unparalleled access to a vast network of investors, mentors, and corporate partners. The programme provides tailored training, business matching, and opportunities for market exposure through exhibitions and trade missions.

Application Process

A rigorous, three-stage vetting process ensures that high-potential ventures are selected. Applications are open year-round.

A Continuous Entrepreneurial Pipeline

Cyberport provides a comprehensive suite of programmes that support startups at every stage of their journey, from initial idea to global expansion.

Idea & Prototype

Cyberport Creative Micro Fund (CCMF) provides HK$100,000 seed funding to turn innovative concepts into working prototypes.

Incubation & Growth

The flagship Incubation Programme offers up to HK$500,000 and comprehensive support to build a viable business.

Acceleration & Expansion

The Accelerator Support Programme (CASP) offers up to HK$300,000 to help startups join global accelerators and enter overseas markets.

Scaling & Investment

The Cyberport Macro Fund (CMF) co-invests in mature startups (Series A and beyond) to fuel their large-scale growth, driving a co-investment ratio of 1:8.
